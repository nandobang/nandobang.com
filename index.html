About me

My name is Fernando, and I'm a fellow web developer living around Porto Alegre city, on the southern region of Brazil. I love technology since I can remember, enticed by videogames and conquered by web development, I am sailing the boat since I was 11.

I've burned my hands on many areas, but my expertise revolves around web sites and applications, ranging from simple one-pagers to complex systems. My abilities also extend to UI and UX design, caring for them as much as I care for my coffee.

Games are part of my life, and I've got my share developing some while in an intern job at a local game studio, working from Flash, through Unity and HTML5.
